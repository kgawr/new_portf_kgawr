🌤️ Weather Analysis

Real weather data for 5 Polish cities fetched from the free Open-Meteo API. Analysis covers temperature trends, precipitation patterns, wind speed and correlations between variables.

Temperature Over Time

Daily min / max / mean temperature — Gdańsk, last 90 days

Key finding: Clear downward trend from late summer into autumn. The gap between daily min and max narrows in October, indicating more overcast days typical of the Baltic coast.

Precipitation

Daily rainfall in mm and rain hours per day

Key finding: Precipitation is irregular and event-driven. Most days have little or no rain, but occasional heavy events (orange/red bars) dominate total monthly figures.

Wind Speed & Gusts

Max daily wind speed vs gusts — Gdańsk

Key finding: Gusts can be 2× the sustained wind speed. Several episodes exceed the 50 km/h strong-wind threshold, consistent with autumn Baltic weather patterns.

City Comparison

Monthly average temperature across 5 Polish cities

Key finding: Wrocław and Poznań tend to be slightly warmer than Gdańsk, which is moderated by proximity to the Baltic Sea. All cities follow the same seasonal pattern.

Correlation Matrix

Pearson correlation between all weather variables

Key finding: Wind speed and gusts are strongly correlated (expected). Precipitation shows weak correlation with temperature — rain occurs across all temperatures in this period.
